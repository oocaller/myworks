Why Join Costco?

Costco offers unbeatable savings, quality products, and amazing employee benefits. Whether you want to shop smart or work in a strong team environment, Costco welcomes you!

Costco Membership Policy

To shop at Costco, customers must have a valid membership. Costco offers two main types of memberships: Gold Star for individuals and Executive for those who want extra benefits like 2% annual rewards. Each membership is valid for one year and can be renewed annually. Members receive a physical or digital card that must be shown at the entrance and checkout. While non-members can browse the store or eat at the food court in some locations, only members can make purchases. Costco’s policy ensures exclusive savings, betterservice, and a trusted shopping experience for all members.

Job Requirements & Employee Treatment

Costco is known for being one of the best employers in the retail industry. To apply for a job, applicants typically need to be at least 18 years old and legally eligible to work in the country. Most positions require strong communication skills, a team-oriented attitude, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment. Prior retail experience is helpful, but not always required.

Costco treats its employees with respect and offers excellent benefits, including competitive wages, paid vacation, health insurance, and opportunities for career advancement. Many employees start in entry-level roles and move up to management positions. Costco’s commitment to fairness and support has made it a top employer in North America.
